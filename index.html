Features

Everything you need to manage money

Built for how Indians actually handle their finances — from EMIs to SIPs, salaries to side income.

📈
Net Worth Tracker
Track 20+ asset categories — FD, gold, stocks, mutual funds, real estate, crypto & more. Add liabilities to see your true net worth.
💳
Expense Tracking
Log every transaction, categorize spending, and see where your money goes each month with visual breakdowns.
🎯
Smart Budgets
Set monthly budgets per category. Get alerts when you're near the limit and track actual vs budgeted spending in real time.
🍯
Savings Goals
Create goals for vacation, car, wedding or anything else. Set monthly contributions and watch your progress with an inflation calculator.
📅
Bill Calendar
Never miss a payment. See all recurring bills on a visual calendar, mark as paid, and get a clear view of what's due this month.
🛡️
Financial Essentials
Track emergency fund, insurance policies, and apply the 50/30/20 rule. A live health checklist shows your financial fitness score.
How It Works

Up and running in under 2 minutes

No tutorials. No complicated setup. Just sign up and start tracking.

1

Create your account

Sign up with your name, email and a password. All data stays on your device — we never see it.

2

Add your finances

Enter transactions, set budgets, add assets & goals. Import from Zerodha, Groww, Upstox or AngelOne via CSV.

3

See the full picture

Your dashboard shows net worth, savings rate, budget health, goal progress and financial fitness score — all live.

Privacy

Built for security & privacy

WealthyBee uses Firebase — the same secure platform trusted by Google — to keep your financial data safe and synced.

🔥
Firebase Secured
Your data is encrypted in transit and stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore. Only you can access your account.
📱
Sync Across Devices
Log in from your phone, tablet, or laptop — your transactions, goals, and assets are always up to date everywhere.
🚫
Zero Ads & Tracking
No analytics, no ad targeting, no third-party tracking. We only use Firebase for your data — nothing else.
📥
Export Anytime
Download your full data as a JSON or CSV backup anytime. Your data, your rules — always portable.
✅ 100% Free — No credit card, no subscriptions, no ads — ever
